About Us


Hack O' Latte provides a platform for developers to solve real-world problems efficiently and effectively. It will be a gathering where programmers code day and night for 36 hrs to come up with new and innovative solutions in the domains of:

• Agriculture
• Socio-Economic Welfare
• Industry 4.0
• Healthcare
• Open Innovation


The goal of Hack O'Latte would be to create a user friendly software at end of event. There would be really cutting-edge problem statements mostly related to our current industrial needs for the competitors to put their thinking caps on! This Hackthon will also allow participants to come up with their own problem statements. Winners would be appreciated with exciting prizes and goodies.


To be organised during Anwesha 2020, at IIT Patna, under DEVELOPER STUDENT CLUBS (DSC) of IIT Patna in association with Anwesha, this is the very first edition of HACK O'LATTE. We are also planning to conduct some guest sessions.

Reach IIT Patna

IIT Patna is located in a town named Bihta in the outskirts of Patna city which is well connected through railways/airways. You can reach IIT Patna through a regular service of buses/trains from Patna which run every half an hour. Look out for buses with codes 888 or 888A at Gandhi Maidan, Patna which will drop you directly at the college. You can also use the local trains which will run from Patna Junction and will drop you at Bihta Station, from where IIT Patna is a short rickshaw ride. During Anwesha, there will be special buses running from Patna to the college the schedule of which can be found on Anwesha's website/facebook page.
